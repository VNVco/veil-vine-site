About Us

Veil & Vine Cleaning Co. is a residential and commercial cleaning business based in Columbus, Ohio, focused on providing personalized, exceptional cleaning services with an emphasis on trust, care, and consistency. Our services include standard recurring cleanings, deep cleanings, move-in/move-out services, and customized commercial packages. The company is committed to using only certified green cleaning products, having thoroughly researched and sourced supplies from manufacturers that align with its core values of safety, sustainability, and environmental responsibility. This eco-conscious approach not only benefits client health and the environment but also positions Veil & Vine as a values-driven service provider in the community.
