Tended Spaces, Trusted Hands

Veil & Vine Cleaning Co. was founded with one belief: every space deserves care that’s both visible and deeply felt. Based in Columbus, Ohio, we offer residential and commercial cleaning with an approach that’s mindful, thorough, and built on trust.

Our team is trained to treat every home, office, or property with the same attention we would give our own. Whether it’s a recurring clean or a one-time deep reset, we ensure your space feels refreshed, peaceful, and well-tended.

As we grow, our vision includes expanding into lawn care — offering full-service solutions that honor both the inside and outside of your sanctuary.
